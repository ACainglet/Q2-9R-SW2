Our Products:

Pandesal

Pan De Manila's bread product, Pandesal,
is one of the best served hot yeast bread rolls.
It is puffy, soft, salty and sweet.

Cheesestick Pesto bread

Cheesestick bread, is also one of the best-selling bread products. It is made from flour, yeast, oil, garlic, especially cheese for toppings. It produces this soft, fluffy feeling, and a tasty aroma.

Tasty whole bread

Tasty bread is a type of whole bread made from flour, yeast, olive oil, including fat. It is soft, fluffy, and tasty when touched and eaten.

Whole Wheat bread

Whole Wheat bread is also a type of a whole bread made from whole wheat flour, all components of grains, and healthy fats. It has also a soft and fluffy composition and a tasty flavoring.

Creamy Peanut Butter

Delicious Pan De Manila Creamy Peanut Butter is a great way to flavor your breakfast or snacks. Sugar, all-natural peanuts, and sugar are used to create its flavorful, creamy texture.

Salted Yema Spread

Yema is a sweet custard delicacy and dessert made with egg yolks, milk, and sugar that is popular and well-likened in the Philippines.

Tsokolate Spread

Pan de Manila Chocolate Spread is a delicious, rich and creamy treat to spread on bread, rolls or toast."

Coconut Jam Spread

Pan De Manila Coconut Spread is a delightful spread from a base of coconut milk, eggs and sugar. Perfect for breakfast or snack.
